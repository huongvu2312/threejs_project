about us

what makes our museum special?

The Museum's extensive expertise in cultural history makes us the leading research museum in Germany. We also have Germany's largest archaeological and ethnographic collections – from the Stone Age, the Viking Age, the Middle Ages and up to recent times. We are one of the first museums to provide a broad, yet integrated perspective of pan-Asian cultures and civilizations.

The international Museum is located in the city center of Berlin. Our tagline is: "where Asian cultures come alive!".
